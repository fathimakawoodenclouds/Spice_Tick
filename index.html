Our Story

A Legacy Rooted in Idukki's Spice Heritage

Nestled in the mist-covered highlands of Idukki, Kerala, Spice Tick brings you the finest spices cultivated by generations of passionate farmers. Our cardamom, pepper, and cloves grow in harmony with nature — nurtured by rich soil, gentle rains, and the cool mountain air of the Western Ghats.

We believe in preserving the authentic flavors that made Kerala the "Spice Garden of India." Every pod, peppercorn, and bud is handpicked with care and delivered with love — straight from our hills to your kitchen.
